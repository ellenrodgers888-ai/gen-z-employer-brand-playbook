# Section 6 — Metrics & Success Measurement

## Success Metrics Framework

Measuring employer brand campaign success requires tracking across three phases: **Awareness → Engagement → Conversion**.

---

## Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

### Phase 1: Awareness
*Are we reaching Gen Z students?*

| Metric | Target | How to Measure |
|--------|--------|----------------|
| **Video Views** | 50,000+ per month | TikTok/Instagram analytics |
| **Reach** | 100,000+ impressions per month | Platform native analytics |
| **Profile Visits** | 1,000+ per week | Platform analytics |
| **Hashtag Impressions** | 500,000+ per campaign | Manual tracking |

---

### Phase 2: Engagement
*Is content resonating?*

| Metric | Target | How to Measure |
|--------|--------|----------------|
| **Video Completion Rate** | 75%+ | Platform analytics (% watched to end) |
| **Engagement Rate** | 8%+ | (Likes + Comments + Shares) / Views |
| **Shares** | 5%+ of views | Platform tracking |
| **Comments** | 50+ per video | Manual count |
| **Save Rate** | 3%+ of views | Platform analytics |
| **Click-Through Rate** | 2%+ to careers page | UTM link tracking |

**Benchmark Context:**
- Average TikTok engagement: 4–6%
- Our target: 8%+ (authentic content should exceed average)
- Strong completion rate indicates hook is working

---

### Phase 3: Conversion
*Are we driving applications?*

| Metric | Target | How to Measure |
|--------|--------|----------------|
| **Careers Page Traffic** | +40% during campaign | Google Analytics |
| **Graduate Applications** | +30% from social campaign | Track UTM parameters |
| **Application Quality** | 20%+ increase | Track average application score |
| **Cost Per Application** | £15–£25 | Marketing spend ÷ applications |
| **Conversion Rate** | 15%+ (views → applications) | Google Analytics funnel |

---

## Data Collection Setup

### Step 1: UTM Tracking
Add tracking parameters to all links:

**TikTok:**
```
https://careers.company.com/graduate?utm_source=tiktok&utm_medium=social&utm_campaign=first-week
```

**LinkedIn:**
```
https://careers.company.com/graduate?utm_source=linkedin&utm_medium=social&utm_campaign=first-week
```

**Instagram:**
```
https://careers.company.com/graduate?utm_source=instagram&utm_medium=social&utm_campaign=first-week
```

This allows you to track exactly which platforms drive applications.

### Step 2: Google Analytics Setup
- Create a dashboard tracking social campaign metrics
- Set up conversion tracking (application form submission)
- Monitor user flow from social → careers page → application

### Step 3: Platform Native Analytics
- Save TikTok analytics weekly (they don't retain history long)
- Screenshot LinkedIn insights monthly
- Export Instagram insights to spreadsheet

## Industry Benchmarks

**What "Good" Looks Like:**

| Metric | Poor | Average | Excellent |
|--------|------|---------|-----------|
| Video Completion | <50% | 60–70% | 75%+ |
| Engagement Rate | <3% | 4–6% | 8%+ |
| CTR to Careers | <1% | 1–2% | 2%+ |
| Application Conversion | <10% | 10–20% | 20%+ |

## Reporting Cadence

### Weekly
- Monitor video performance
- Check careers page traffic trends
- Identify top-performing content

### Monthly
- Full dashboard review
- Benchmark against targets
- Analyze top-performing content types
- Share insights with team

### Quarterly
- Comprehensive campaign review
- ROI calculation
- Strategy adjustments for next quarter
- Present findings to stakeholders

---

## Sample ROI Calculation

```
Campaign Budget: £5,000
Total Applications: 245
Total Hires from Campaign: 60 (25% conversion)

Cost Per Hire: £83 (£5,000 ÷ 60)
Average Salary: £35,000

ROI Calculation:
- First-year value per hire: £35,000
- Campaign cost: £5,000
- ROI: (£35,000 - £5,000) / £5,000 = 600% ✅

Plus intangible value:
- Brand awareness in 100,000+ students
- Employer brand lift across sector
- Employee referral network strengthened
```

## Continuous Improvement

### Analyze & Iterate
- Which content types drive highest engagement?
- Which platforms drive highest-quality applicants?
- What messaging resonates most?
- Where do applicants drop off?

### A/B Testing Ideas
- Test different hook phrases
- Test posting times
- Test video lengths
- Test thumbnail styles

### Feedback Loop
- Monthly team retrospective
- Student feedback (post-event surveys)
- Recruiter observations
- Application quality trends

---

## Final Note

**The goal isn't vanity metrics—it's sustainable talent acquisition.**

Focus on:
✅ Quality of applicants, not just quantity
✅ Long-term employer brand building
✅ Authentic engagement over inflated numbers
✅ Measurable business impact (hires, retention)
