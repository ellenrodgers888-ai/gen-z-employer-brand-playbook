# Gen Z Employer Brand Playbook 🎯

This project explores creative marketing strategies to attract Gen Z audiences to graduate programmes in finance, technology, and startups.

**Created as part of my interest in early careers marketing and employer branding.**

---

## 🎯 Objective

Understand how companies can attract Gen Z students and graduates to early career programmes through social media and digital content.

## 👥 Target Audience

University students aged 18–24 interested in careers in:
- Finance & Fintech
- Technology
- Startups

## 🔍 Key Findings

- Gen Z discovers roles through **social media, not job boards**
- **Authentic employee content** outperforms traditional corporate messaging
- **Short-form video** (TikTok, Reels) drives highest engagement
- **Peer recommendations** remain the strongest trust signal
