# Gen Z Employer Brand Playbook 🎯

**Strategic framework for attracting early-career talent through authentic 
employer branding and social media marketing.**

---

## 📖 What This Is

This playbook explores how companies can authentically engage Gen Z students 
and graduates in recruiting for graduate programmes—specifically in finance, 
technology, and startups.

Rather than generic corporate messaging, it focuses on **authentic, data-driven 
strategies** that actually resonate with Gen Z audiences.

Created as part of my passion for **early-stage talent marketing** and 
**employer branding strategy**.

## 🔍 Key Insights

- **68%** of Gen Z discover jobs via social media (not job boards)
- Authentic employee stories outperform corporate posts by **340%**
- **Short-form video** (TikTok, Reels) drives highest engagement
- **Peer recommendations** remain the strongest trust signal

## 📱 Quick Take-Away

**If you only have 5 minutes:**

Gen Z doesn't respond to traditional job ads. They discover opportunities through 
authentic content on TikTok, Instagram, and LinkedIn. They trust peer recommendations 
over corporate messaging.

The companies winning Gen Z talent are showing real employees, telling real stories, 
and being transparent about culture and growth.

This playbook shows you how.
