# Section 4 — Campaign Idea

## Campaign Concept: "First Week in Fintech"

A short-form video series following new interns during their first week at the company.

**Campaign Goal:** 
Show authentic, relatable entry into the workplace while building excitement around internship applications.

**Format:** 5-part TikTok/Instagram Reels series (30 seconds each)

---

## Episode Breakdown

### Episode 1: "Expectation vs Reality: First Day"

**Hook:** "What I thought my first day would be vs what actually happened"

**Scene 1 (Expectation):**
- Imagined: Formal boardroom, intimidating boss, perfect outfit
- Voiceover: "I was SO nervous..."

**Scene 2 (Reality):**
- Actual: Casual dress code, friendly team, coffee machine tour
- Voiceover: "...but it was nothing like that"

**CTA:** "Want to experience your first week? Applications open [date]"

---

### Episode 2: "Three Things That Surprised Me"

**Surprise 1:** "Nobody knew the answer to everything—and that's okay"
- Shows someone asking for help, getting support

**Surprise 2:** "My team actually wanted me to succeed"
- Shows mentorship moment, genuine support

**Surprise 3:** "I made mistakes, and nobody fired me"
- Shows a funny blooper/learning moment

**CTA:** "Ready to be surprised? Apply now [link]"

---

### Episode 3: "The Unspoken Rules I Learned"

- Team lunch traditions
- How to ask for help
- When to jump into meetings vs listen
- Coffee order culture
- Desk setup rituals

**Tone:** Humorous, relatable, insider knowledge

**CTA:** "Want to learn these secrets? [Link]"

---

### Episode 4: "What I'm Actually Learning"

- Technical skills being gained
- Real projects touched
- Mentorship received
- Industry knowledge picked up

**This one is more serious/informative.**

**CTA:** "This could be you. Applications [open/closing soon]"

---

### Episode 5: "One Month In: What Changed"

- Day 1 me vs Day 30 me
- Confidence gained
- Skills developed
- Team connections formed

**Emotional note:** "It was scary, it was exciting, and I'm so glad I did it."

**CTA:** "Apply for next year's program [link]"

---

## Distribution Strategy

**TikTok:**
- Post Monday–Friday
- Use trending sounds
- Hashtags: #FirstWeekInFintech #Fintech #InternLife #GraduateRecuitment
- Tag company account

**Instagram Reels:**
- Post to main feed 2x per week
- Stories daily teasers
- Hashtags: Same as TikTok + industry-specific tags

**LinkedIn:**
- Post longer version (1–2 min) with professional framing
- Caption: "Demystifying the first week of a fintech internship"
- Share with employees to amplify

---

## Expected Performance

| Metric | Target |
|--------|--------|
| Video completion rate | 75%+ |
| Average engagement rate | 8%+ |
| Link clicks to careers page | 5%+ |
| Application uplift | +30% week-of campaign |

---

## Campaign Timeline

- **Week 1:** Episode 1 & 2 (build awareness)
- **Week 2:** Episode 3 & 4 (maintain momentum)
- **Week 3:** Episode 5 + retrospective post (close strong)
- **Week 4:** Repurpose into long-form blog/LinkedIn article

---

## Why This Works

✅ Authentic employee voice (actual interns, not actors)
✅ Addresses real student anxieties
✅ Shows culture through action, not words
✅ Creates FOMO (fear of missing out on the experience)
✅ Highly shareable and relatable
✅ Generates user-generated content (students sharing)
