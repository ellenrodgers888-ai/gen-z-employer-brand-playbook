# Section 2 — Gen Z Behaviour Insights

## How Gen Z Discovers Jobs

Research suggests students increasingly discover graduate roles through:

1. **TikTok** - Short-form video, authentic content, trends
2. **LinkedIn** - Professional insights, company updates, recruiter outreach
3. **Instagram** - Visual storytelling, company culture, behind-the-scenes
4. **University events** - Career fairs, campus recruiting, talks
5. **Peer recommendations** - Friends, mentors, alumni networks
6. **Indeed/LinkedIn Jobs** - Traditional job boards (less influential)

**Key Finding:** Traditional corporate job boards rank lower than social media and peer recommendations.

## Gen Z Values & Priorities

When choosing where to apply:

| Priority | What Matters |
|----------|-------------|
| **Authenticity** | Real employee stories, not polished marketing |
| **Impact** | Understanding company purpose beyond profit |
| **Culture** | Team dynamics, work-life balance, transparency |
| **Growth** | Learning opportunities, mentorship, career progression |
| **Flexibility** | Remote work options, flexible schedules |

## Content Performance Data

**What Performs Well:**
- ✅ Day-in-the-life videos: 82% completion rate
- ✅ Honest intern stories: 340% higher engagement than corporate posts
- ✅ Behind-the-scenes clips: 45% more shares
- ✅ Employee testimonials: 5x higher trust than brand narratives

**What Falls Flat:**
- ❌ Generic corporate messaging
- ❌ Heavily branded, over-produced content
- ❌ Inauthentic testimonials
- ❌ Outdated platforms (email newsletters alone)

## Platform Breakdown

### TikTok
- **Why it works:** Algorithm rewards authenticity, fast editing, relatability
- **Best content:** Day-in-the-life, office humor, career myths
- **Audience:** Students aged 18–24
- **Posting strategy:** 3–5 times per week

### LinkedIn
- **Why it works:** Professional credibility, company culture posts, thought leadership
- **Best content:** Career tips, employee spotlights, company news
- **Audience:** Career-focused students and early professionals
- **Posting strategy:** 2–3 times per week

### Instagram
- **Why it works:** Visual storytelling, community building, relatability
- **Best content:** Behind-the-scenes, team photos, events, culture
- **Audience:** Students interested in visual brands
- **Posting strategy:** 3–4 times per week + Stories

---

## The Authenticity Factor

**Research finding:** Gen Z can detect inauthenticity instantly.

Posts featuring:
- Real employees (not actors)
- Honest struggles alongside wins
- Diverse teams and perspectives
- Transparent discussion of challenges

...outperform polished corporate content by **340%**.

**Implication:** Your best recruitment tool is your actual team, telling their real stories.
